About Abefe Motors

Our Story

Abefe Motors was founded with a simple mission: to provide quality vehicles and exceptional customer service to Nigerians. Over the years, we've grown from a small Lagos dealership to one of Nigeria's most trusted automotive retailers, serving customers across the country.

Why Choose Us?

  • Extensive inventory of brand new and Tokunbo (foreign-used) vehicles
  • Flexible payment plans tailored for Nigerians
  • Full-service maintenance and repair center
  • Free vehicle documentation assistance
  • Nationwide delivery available
  • 90-day warranty on all vehicles

Our Values

  1. Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
  2. Quality: Every vehicle meets our rigorous standards
  3. Service: Customer satisfaction is our top priority
  4. Community: We're proud to serve Nigeria and support our local communities
