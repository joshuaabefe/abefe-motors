Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring for the test drive?

Please bring a valid Nigerian driver's license and a form of identification. We also recommend bringing proof of address if you're planning to proceed with a purchase.

Can someone accompany me during the test drive?

Yes! You're welcome to bring one or two people with you. However, only the person who booked the test drive (with a valid license) can drive the vehicle.

How long does a test drive take?

A typical test drive lasts between 30-45 minutes. This includes time for vehicle demonstration, the actual driving experience, and a brief consultation about financing options.

What if I want to test drive multiple vehicles?

Absolutely! We encourage it. Please mention this when our team contacts you, and we'll schedule adequate time for you to test drive multiple vehicles.

Is there any cost for a test drive?

No, test drives are completely free at Abefe Motors. There's no obligation to purchase.
